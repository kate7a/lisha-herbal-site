Our Story

Founded in 2010 by herbalist Eliza Chen, Lisha Herbals began as a small workshop dedicated to creating effective natural remedies using traditional herbal knowledge combined with modern scientific research.

Our journey started with a simple mission: to harness nature's healing power in its purest form. Over the years, we've grown from a local favorite to an internationally recognized brand, but our commitment to quality, sustainability, and efficacy remains unchanged.

Every Lisha product is still crafted in small batches to ensure quality, using ingredients sourced from trusted organic farmers and wild-crafters around the world. Our formulations are developed through a careful blend of ancient wisdom and contemporary research, creating products that truly work in harmony with your body.
